Where you're losing visibility

  • Review volume: 12 reviews vs the top-3 average of 87. Google weighs volume heavily, the single biggest closeable gap.
  • Rating threshold: 4.1★ sits below the 4.3★ filter for the top-3 pack. Leaders average 4.8★.
  • Local pack position: #9, page 1, but outside the top 3 that capture ~70% of clicks.

Three levers that would move the needle

  • Review velocity: a structured monthly collection system plus responses to every existing review.
  • GBP post + service cadence: Google rewards weekly fresh content; your service list needs to map to specialty searches.
  • Citation footprint + NAP alignment: consistent name/address/phone across 30–40 directories, a silent ceiling if mismatched.
