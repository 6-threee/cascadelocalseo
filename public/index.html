FAQ

How does the audit actually work?

You submit your business. My system pulls your current rank, review profile, and the top 3 competitors in your area for your primary keyword. I write up three specific gaps and three specific actions. You get the audit by email, usually within 24 hours, sometimes within an hour.

Why is the audit free?

Two reasons. First, it's mostly automated — it costs me about a dollar to generate. Second, most cold sales feel terrible because they ask before they give. I'd rather give first. If the audit is useful and you want help executing on it, hire me. If not, no harm done.

I'm not a dentist/lawyer/HVAC/plumber/roofer. Can you still help?

Probably yes, but the automated benchmarks I show on this page only cover those industries right now. If you're a vet, a med spa, an auto shop, a real estate agent, anything with local search demand — submit anyway. The audit logic adapts.

Are you actually in Central Oregon?

Yes. I work remotely but operate locally. Most of my clients are in the Bend/Redmond corridor.

How fast can I expect results?

For most local pack improvements: 30–90 days. Reviews compound; one strong review month moves the needle. Top-3 ranking changes typically happen in months 2–4 once the basics are fixed. If your business is brand new with no GBP listing, the timeline doubles. I'll be honest about timing in your audit.

What if I already have an SEO agency?

Get the audit anyway. Use it to ask your agency hard questions. If they're doing their job, they'll appreciate the second set of eyes. If not, you'll know.

Will you spam me?

No. The audit comes once. If you don't reply, you'll get one short check-in a week later and one final note a week after that. Then I stop and you never hear from me again. Three emails total, max.

Do you sign contracts?

Month-to-month only. If I can't keep delivering value, you should be able to leave.
