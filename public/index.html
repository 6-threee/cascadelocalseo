Local SEO in the AI era

Search changed. AI Overviews answer before anyone scrolls, and more people just ask an assistant to "find me a good [dentist] nearby." Here's the part most owners miss: those AI answers are built from the same signals as the map pack — your Google Business Profile, your review momentum, your structured data, and how consistent your business details are across the web.

Get those right and you win both surfaces. Get them wrong and you're invisible in the one that's growing fastest. So every audit now includes an AI-visibility read:

  • Profile + categories — the source of truth an AI reads before it recommends anyone.
  • Review velocity, recency & sentiment — what "best [service] near me" answers weigh most.
  • Structured data & entity consistency — so machines can parse you and pick you with confidence.
  • AI-answer presence — whether you actually show up when someone asks Google's AI for your service.

Most local SEO still chases "rank #1 on Google." I tune for getting found and recommended wherever your customers now look — the map pack and AI answers alike.
