# Cascade Local SEO — Reel scripts (launch month, 1/week)

Faceless, screen-recording + text-on-motion. Honors the no-talking-photos rule (real motion only). Each Reel: a rendered hook/cover frame (in `social-assets/`) opens it, you screen-record the middle per the beats, and a simple end card closes it. Keep 20-30s, continuous motion, on-screen captions always on. Post Thursdays before 11 AM PT.

How to assemble (CapCut / IG editor): drop the cover frame as the first 1-2s, layer the on-screen text beats over your screen recording, end on the CTA text. Add quiet trending audio. Caption goes in the post (front-load the keyword in the first line).
