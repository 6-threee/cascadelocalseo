## 0. The strategy (read this first)

For a B2B local-SEO agency, Instagram is **not a lead firehose**. Med spa owners and chiropractors do not buy a retainer off a Reel. So this account has three jobs, in priority order:

1. **Credibility backstop.** When the `ig-dm-discover` engine sends a cold DM and the prospect clicks the profile, a real, valuable, on-brand grid is the difference between "real operator" and "ignore." This is job #1.
2. **Warm-up vehicle.** Following and genuinely engaging target businesses warms the account so the cold-DM engine can go live without tripping spam systems.
3. **Slow inbound authority.** Useful local-SEO content compounds into trust over quarters, not weeks.

Everything below is sized to that truth. One hard constraint: **0 paying clients yet, so no fabricated case studies, logos, testimonials, or invented numbers.** Proof comes from the public Power Rankings leaderboard (real data, 118 boards), general industry stats, and education. The honesty is part of the brand.

**4 content pillars (rotate):** (1) Found AND Recommended (AI-era wedge) · (2) Plain-English education · (3) Proof done honestly · (4) Founder / agent-run model.

## 1. Format + algorithm playbook (2026)

**What the algorithm weighs now, in order:** watch time (first 3 seconds decide everything) > sends/shares (the heaviest "spread this" signal) > saves (future-utility, the natural strength of a teaching account) > likes. Reach is no longer fixed per post; a post that flops with followers can still get pushed to strangers if watch-time and sends hold. **Optimize for sends + saves + 3-second retention, not likes.**

**Format priorities:**
- **Carousels = the priority.** Highest engagement and saves of any format; they signal expertise. This is what a DM-prospect clicking through needs to see. Our close.
- **Reels = reach/discovery.** Best tool for finding strangers and the businesses we want to engage. Our net.
- **Stories = warm-up + retention.** Where the warm-up job actually happens (sticker taps are high-value micro-engagement).
- **Single images = filler.** Stat cards, quotes, announcements. Not a growth lever.

**Cadence (sustainable for a solo + agents):** 2 carousels/week + 1-2 faceless Reels/week + 3-5 stories/week. Roughly 3-4 feed posts/week. If it gets heavy, cut Reels before carousels. Consistency beats volume.

**Instagram SEO (the 2026 shift that favors us):** IG now behaves like a search engine and public pro-account posts can surface in Google.
- Write **searchable captions** with the exact phrases owners type ("why my business isn't showing in Google Maps," "get found in ChatGPT").
- **Front-load the keyword** in the first 125 characters (the visible preview).
- Set the searchable **Name field** to `Cascade Local SEO | Get Found + Recommended` (separate from the @handle, this is what surfaces in search).

**Hashtags:** dead as a discovery engine. Use **3-5 niche tags** per post (#localseo, #googlebusinessprofile, #mappack + one vertical tag). Skip mega-tags. The 20-tag dump reads as spam now.

**Faceless Reels (fits the agent-run model, honors the no-talking-photos rule):**
- **Screen-recording walkthroughs are our strongest play.** Record the `/rankings` leaderboard, a live map-pack search, a GBP, or an AI answer naming a local business. Annotate. Real data, zero cost.
- Text-on-motion explainers (15-30s, one idea).
- Real motion only, continuous visual change, stitch short cuts, 15-45s.

**Best times (US service-business owners):** weekdays 9-11 AM and 1-3 PM local. Post carousels 9 AM-12 PM, stories 11 AM-1 PM. Avoid nights/weekends as primary slots. Anchor to Pacific but skew earlier to catch Eastern. **Override with your own Insights after ~30 days.**

_Confidence notes: the "1 send ≈ 15 likes" weighting is an industry estimate, not a Meta figure. Exact engagement percentages vary by study; the direction (carousels win saves, Reels win reach for small accounts) is consistent. Best-times are aggregate, validate against our Insights._

## 4. Growth + engagement engine

### Weeks 1-2 warm-up protocol (BEFORE switching on ig-dm-discover)
Seed 6-9 grid pieces first (an empty grid converts no one). Then:
- **Week 1/day:** follow 8-12 ICP accounts, 5-8 specific genuine comments, 15-20 likes, reply to 100% of inbound same-day. Spread across a 9 AM and a 2 PM block, never one burst.
- **Week 2/day:** follow 12-18, comment 8-10, like 20-30, start resharing 1-2 posts to stories.
- **Targets:** ICP business hashtags (#medspa #chiropractor #dentistlife #hvaclife #plumberlife #roofingcontractor #salonowner #autorepairshop) + owner tags + a slice of peer/SEO accounts. Prioritize active accounts over big dead ones.
- **Avoid flags:** stay far under limits (~150 follows/day, 300-500 likes/day are ceilings, you sit well below). No copy-paste comments, no follow/unfollow churn, no links in comments. If you see "action blocked," stop all actions 24-48h.
- **Gate to go live:** 2 full weeks, zero action-blocks, 9+ grid posts, some organic follow-backs. If any is missing, extend a week. (Cold DMs still send manually per the engine's design.)

### Once live: daily (~15 min) + weekly (~45 min)
- **Daily:** reply to all comments/DMs same-day; 8-12 specific comments; 20-30 likes; follow 10-15 new ICP accounts (doubles as DM-target warm-up); reshare 1 useful post to stories.
- **Weekly:** review which formats/topics got saves/shares/profile-clicks; engage in 3-5 larger threads; audit new followers for ICP fit; check link-in-bio clicks + profile visits; queue next week from the repurpose engine.

### The funnel tie-in
1. Credibility backstop for DMs (job #1). 2. Link-in-bio → free audit (cascadelocalseo.com). 3. Future: add the 15-min booking link as a second bio link when live. 4. The `/rankings` leaderboard is the bridge: the one shareable proof asset at 0 clients, used in both posts and DM follow-ups.

### Repurpose engine (never create from scratch)
| Source | IG output |
|---|---|
| Each blog post | 3-4 takeaways → carousel; one stat → single post |
| Each audit insight (anonymized) | "What we found on a real med spa's GBP" teaching carousel (no names, no fake numbers) |
| Each LinkedIn post (Jonathan's) | reformat as IG caption + simple graphic / Reel |
| The /rankings leaderboard | "Top 3 in [city] all do these 3 things" carousel + story |
| A "found AND recommended" idea | short explainer Reel |
| A common DM question | one question → one answer post |

Rule: anonymize, never fabricate.

### Honest 90-day trajectory + KPIs
| Milestone | Followers | Reality |
|---|---|---|
| Day 30 | 80-150 | Mostly outbound follows + follow-backs + peers. Grid established, looks legit. |
| Day 60 | 200-350 | Engagement compounding, first saves/shares, some ICP follow-backs. |
| Day 90 | 350-600 | Credible profile, a few inbound audit clicks. Still primarily a DM backstop. |

A chunk will be peers, not buyers. That's fine. **400 of-the-right-people beats 4,000 random.**

**The one metric that matters: profile visits → link-in-bio clicks** (and DM-prospect profile clicks), NOT follower count. If profile visits rise but clicks don't, fix the bio/proof, not reach.

| KPI | 90-day target |
|---|---|
| Profile visits / week | 40-120 |
| Link-in-bio clicks / week | 3-10 (low is normal for B2B) |
| Saves + shares per educational post | 3-15 |
| Comment/DM response rate | 100% same-day |
| Followers (sanity check, not north star) | 350-600 |
